About Half Moon

Established in 1926 as "The Terminal," Half Moon has been a cornerstone of Brighton's community. In 2003, the Morris Group revitalized this historic venue, blending its rich heritage with modern hospitality. Today, Half Moon stands as a testament to enduring traditions and contemporary excellence, offering a welcoming atmosphere for all.
